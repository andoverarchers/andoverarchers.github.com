7th August 2012 - Members of Andover Archers part of overall Hampshire team triumph at Salisbury Double FITA 70.

On Sunday 5th August 2012, as part of the Salisbury Double FITA 70 shoot, an inter-county match was held between Dorset & Wiltshire and Hampshire.

Three archers from Andover were selected to be part of the Hampshire Team. Paul Talmage was again selected for the Gents Recurve while Abbie Loader who had made two previous appearances for the Hampshire Junior team was selected to make her first appearance for the Senior Team. Steph Watson made her first ever appearance for the County when selected for the Ladies Compound.

In the absence of Gill Millward, Nick Loader took over as Team Manager for the day.

It was a really close match overall with DWAA winning the Gents Recurve and Ladies Compound but Hampshire fighting back to win the Gents Compound and Ladies Recurve.

Overall, Hampshire triumphed by just 33 points with the total scores 13647 for DWAA against 13680 for Hampshire and it was a proud moment for Nick when he received the Trophy on behalf of the Team.

There was more good news for Andover in the results of the shoot itself with Abbie winning the Under 20s Junior award, beating her own County Record by no less than 103 points in the morning session. Not to be outdone, Vicky Loader won the under 17s Cadet award in her first ever attemp at 60m!

19th June 2012 - Carl Mayers awarded OBE.

Dr Carl Mayers, OBE!

Our club Records Officer, Carl Mayers, has been awarded an OBE in the Queen's Birthday Honours list, for services to the Ministry of Defence.

From the Salisbury Journal:

Two Porton Down scientists are among the local people who have been recognised in the Queen’s Birthday Honours list.

Scientists Dr Paul Rice and Dr Carl Mayers, who are based at the Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (Dstl) at Porton Down, have been awarded OBEs for their work helping soldiers in Afghanistan.

Dr Mayers has been awarded the OBE in recognition of his work as a biologist. He recently developed a pioneering screening capability and training programme which has had a significant impact for the UK military in Afghanistan.

Dr Mayers said: “It’s been a wonderful surprise to receive this honour. I consider myself very fortunate to work with such a talented and dedicated team of people and I’m extremely proud of the impact our work has had.”

The club extends its warmest congratulations to Carl!

27th May 2012 - Andover Archers compete and win at Southampton H2H.

This weekend saw Southampton Archery Club host their World Record Status FITA 70 and Olympic Style Head to Head competition.

Andover was represented by Paul Talmage in the Gent's Recurve, Steve Wrigley in the Gent's Compound and Steph Watson in the ladies Compound but the highlight for the Club was the performance of Sally Allan and Abbie Loader in the Ladies Recurve!

On Saturday, after the morning qualifying round, Sally was seeded 3 and Abbie was seeded 7. In the afternoon H2H knockout section, both archers reached the Semi Final stage where they were drawn against each other with Sally being victorious. Sally went on to win the event while Abbie won the shoot out to finish 3rd! Abigail also set a new Hampshire County Record for the 70m section.

On Sunday, after the morning qualifying round, Sally was seeded 2 and Abbie was again seeded 7. This time, they faced each other in the Quarter Final with Sally again being victorious. Like Saturday, Sally again went on to win the tournament beating the number 1 seed in the Final.

The Club should be very proud of the performance of all of our archers who took part as they have made a tremendous contribution to enhancing the reputation of Andover Archers!

24th April 2012 - New online database of club scores.

A message from Carl, our club records officer:

Hello All,

We're introducing a new system where all the scores submitted to the Records Officer (that's me!) will be recorded on a database that you can access on the internet. You'll be able to view all the scores as I enter them, and we'll always have a back up of all the scores if there are any problems.

You can access all the scores here.

The scores that are entered at the moment are those I've received from the club, and I intend to enter the results from shoots that people have attended as I get more time. If you leave results in the pigeonhole in the club house, I'll enter them for you. In time I will get the club records on here too, and enter your scores from previous years.

One quick request - please make sure you record scores, names and dates correctly and clearly on your score sheets, and do yell at me if I make any mistakes entering them - they're easily fixed!

After a bit more work this page will also tell you your GNAS handicap and classification - I'll let you know when that's added.

Cheers, Carl.

5th February 2012 - Andover Junior Archers excel at Talisman shoot

Junior Talismen

12 juniors from Andover were entered for the 2012 Talisman competition on Sunday 5th February. Sadly, the overnight bad weather forced 4 of them to withdraw on the morning of the shoot. The remaining 8 young archers produced one of the finest performances by Andover Archers for many years.

Harry Welfare took Gold in the Under 18 Gents Recurve.

Abbie Loader took Gold in the Under 18 Ladies Recurve.

William Talmage took Gold in the Under 16 Gents Compound.

Adam Portsmouth took Gold in the Under 14 Gents Recurve.

Jonathan Smith took Silver in the Under 14 Gents Recurve.

Vicky Loader took Gold in the Under 14 Ladies Recurve.

Jamie Welfare took Gold in the junior Gents Longbow.

Michael Russell took Gold in the Under 12 Gents Recurve.

If all that was not enough, Harry, Abbie and Adam also won Silver medals in the recurve team event, and Harry added another Gold for the "Best Gold".

-Nick
