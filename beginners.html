Beginners courses

Andover Archers run around three or four courses per year depending on demand. On each course we can accommodate up to twelve people.

The course will consist of six two hour sessions run on Thursday evenings, 7 p.m. to 9 p.m.

Your training will cover aspects of all modern bow types, arrows and accessories, shooting technique, scoring, safety and the rules of shooting (with the exception of cross bows).

All training is provided by accredited coaches and experienced club members.

Andover Archers has a large selection of club equipment and all of the necessary equipment will be provided.

All archers completing the course to a suitable standard will be issued with a GNAS recognised certificate of competency, which will enable the holder to apply for membership of any archery club.

Andover Archers is a wheelchair friendly club, and will accept archers of all abilities.

The cost for the course is £50.00, which includes all tuition, insurance and equipment hire for the duration of the course.

If you are interested in attending an Andover Archers beginners course, please email the Secretary (details on contacts page of this website) with details of your name, address, telephone number and email address.

Please also let us know if you have any special requirements/disabilities that are pertinent to your attendance on the course. Your details will be held on record, in confidence, and, once dates have been confirmed, applicants will be notified by email along with the necessary joining instructions and directions to the club.

Should you have any immediate questions, please email the club secretary.
