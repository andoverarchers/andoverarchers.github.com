Members

This page contains information specific to members of the club, such as the club's constitution and forms that are required in certain circumstances.

Club Constitution

The club's constitution can be downloaded here. It details the aims of the club, how it is formally run, and the responsibilities of the members and committee members. The constitution is currently under review, but the version linked here is correct as of November 2011.

A paper copy of the constitution can be found display on the noticeboard in the clubhouse.

Membership Renewal Form

Every year, each member of the club must fill in the membership renewal form, in order that contact details are kept up to date. It can be downloaded here.

Junior Email and Texting Parent Consent Form

In accordance with official Archery GB guidelines, any junior members of the club who wish to be contacted directly by the club via email or text message must have permission from their parents or guardians, via this form. An opt-out from such communications is also available. Further information can be found on the cover page of the form, which is downloadable here.
