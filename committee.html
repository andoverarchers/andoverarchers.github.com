The Committee

The club committee is a group of elected members, who work together to organise the club's day-to-day operations. The committee meets regularly, and their meetings will be advertised via this website. Similarly, the minutes of past committee meetings will appear on this page.

Committee members are elected annually at the club AGM. Further information on the role and function of the committee can be found in the club's constitution.

Executive Members

The "executive" committee positions are the Chairman, Secretary and Treasurer. At least two executive members of the committee must be present at a committee meeting, out of an overall quorum of four.

Co-opted Club Members

The committee may from time to time decide to co-opt members of the club into a formal role, additional to the normal committee positions. Their position is officially ended at each AGM, although they can be co-opted again at the same meeting with the agreement of the membership.

The co-opted members are not formally part of the committee, and cannot vote at committee meetings. However, they are listed here for completeness and to recognise their contribution to the club.
