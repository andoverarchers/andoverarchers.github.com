Tournaments

Home Tournaments

The club organises three major tournaments annually, details of which can be found below.

'Saxon' Shoot

The 2012 'Saxon' shoot took place on the 29th April. Unfortunately, the shoot had to be abandoned due to the weather after 6 dozen arrows. The results after 6 dozen arrows can be downloaded here.

Spring FITA Star 2012

Our Spring FITA star took place on Sunday, 13th May 2012. The results can be downloaded here.

ANDOBA Double FITA Star

The ANDOBA Double FITA Star has been running for over 20 years. It is run as a combined event by Andover Archers together with Old Basing Archers. Further information can be found at the official webpage.

The target list for the 2012 ANDOBA competition can be downloaded here.
